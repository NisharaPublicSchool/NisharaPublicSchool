Nishara Group of Institutions

A Unit of Hydrose Haji Memorial Charitable Trust, Registered in Bengaluru in the Year 2000

About the Institution

The Trust manages various educational institutions:

  • Nishara Public School (CBSE, New Delhi)
  • Nishara Educational Society (Karnataka State Syllabus)
  • Nishara Pre-University College (Karnataka State Syllabus)
  • Nishara Degree College (Affiliated to Mangalore University)
  • Raziya Health Care and Training Centre

These institutions were founded under the guidance of Shri B. Abdul Razak, with a vision to provide quality education and healthcare services to society.

Commitment to Society

  • The trust reserves 25% of seats for free education under the Right to Education Act.
  • An additional 15% of total strength receives free education through the trust.
  • All students receive free mid-day meals, while KG students are provided morning snacks, milk, and biscuits.
  • A dedicated clinic facility is available for medical emergencies through Raziya Health Care.
  • Free medical facilities are provided to students and parents through health cards distributed by the trust.
  • To ensure quality education reaches rural areas, a reasonable and affordable fee structure is maintained.

Awards & Recognition

Over the past ten years, Nishara Group of Institutions has grown immensely and has been honored with the prestigious "Kitturu Rani Chennamma Award" by the state government for its dedicated service to society.

Love and Care in Education

At Nishara, we believe that strong teacher-student relationships are built on genuine care. Our teachers not only prepare students for future achievements but also foster positive values in them. We focus on making learning fun, meaningful, and enduring.

Physical Activities & Sports

Health and physical education are essential to a student’s development. We engage students in various activities:

  • Outdoor games like football, volleyball, throwball, cricket, badminton, kabaddi, and kho-kho.
  • Other activities such as Karate, Yoga, Dance, and Campus Cleaning Campaigns.

Nutritious & Delicious Food

To improve students’ nutrition, we provide organic foods and natural drinks. Free mid-day meals are given to all children, and KG students receive snacks, milk, and biscuits.

Safe & Reliable Transportation

Our school provides daily transport with a fleet of well-maintained buses operating in Mangalore. Each bus is driven by a trained driver and has a female caregiver to ensure the safety of students.

Academic Excellence

At Nishara, we recognize that every child is unique. Our curriculum focuses on academic excellence while promoting moral and spiritual development to shape responsible and mature individuals.

Experienced Faculty

Our faculty consists of 35 well-trained teachers who undergo continuous professional development to bring out the best in each student.
